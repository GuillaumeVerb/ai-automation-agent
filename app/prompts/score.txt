Assess automation readiness for a human-in-the-loop workflow.

Return:
- automation_score: integer from 0 to 100
- risk_level: low | medium | high
- estimated_time_saved_minutes: integer
- autonomy_recommendation: ready_for_assisted | human_review | manual_only
- rationale: short explanation

Instructions:
- Lower the score when confidence is weak or priority is high.
- High-risk cases should stay human reviewed.
- Keep rationale concise and operational.
