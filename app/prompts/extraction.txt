Extract a strict JSON object for an internal operations copilot.

Fields:
- priority: low | medium | high
- subject: concise business subject line
- deadline: string or null
- actor: string or null
- action_requested: prepare_reply | prepare_report | triage_issue | assess_request
- channel: email | text | json
- tone: urgent | polite | neutral

Instructions:
- Use null when a value is not grounded in the request.
- Do not invent names, deadlines, or facts.
- Keep subject short and readable.
